Small and mid-size companies often have trouble
finding suitable office space in Amsterdam. The
creative industry has seemingly unrealistic
demands when it comes to office locations: an
incubator of ideas, which is spacious and inspiring,
with a unique design at a great location with car
access. Salt is a response to these needs, providing
small, high quality offices, each with a unique
identity.
